ABOUT

In 1318, all houses of the Racibórz burghers had the right to brew beer. The brewing privilege was the most important source of income. At that time, beer was not brewed all year round, only from autumn to spring.

In 2005, the brewery was re-launched. Despite its turbulent history, it was possible to preserve not only this unique facility, but also the tradition of producing the golden drink. Currently, the Castle Brewery is celebrating its 450th anniversary.

"We create beer, others make it."

Opening hours: Monday-Saturday from 7am to 3pm.

Address: Zamkowa 2B, 47-400 Racibórz
